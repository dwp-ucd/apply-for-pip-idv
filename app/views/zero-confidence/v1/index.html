Prototype

We tested the new zero confidence user journey for Apply for PIP. We skipped PIP1 and PIP2 questions and asked participants to continue with confirming their identity from the 'Stage 2 submitted' page.

We continued with the OIDV unhappy path screens.

We also tested the updated versions of email and text OIDV reminders.
